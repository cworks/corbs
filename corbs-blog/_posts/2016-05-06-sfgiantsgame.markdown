Why yes we are going to San Fransisco and on a whim really but boy did we hit the jackpot at the ballgame.  I've long wanted to go to AT&T park despite my bad memories from the [2010 World Series](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2010_World_Series) when the Giants beat my beloved Rangers (I would have my revenge though).

Without any planning we walked right into [Willie Mays](http://www.baseball-reference.com/players/m/mayswi01.shtml) 85th birthday celebration and if thats not enough it was Metallica night!  Metallica national anthem...check, Metallica jamming Happy Birthday to Willie...check, wear my Texas Rangers garb at a Giants vs. Rockies game check!

Had a great time at a [great park](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AT%26T_Park) and yes I'll admit...a great ball-club with lots of history.  It was a cool night which I enjoyed because you rarely if ever get to wear long sleeves at a Rangers game.  The food was good, atmosphere great and the fans I'll have to say know their baseball.  [Giants beat Rockies 6-4](http://www.baseball-reference.com/boxes/SFN/SFN201605060.shtml) in a come from behind win.

I do have to say I was a bit disappointed that only one person out of 40K said something about my Rangers garb.  The stadium was filled with black and orange and I alone wore Rangers red and blue.  No one around us said a thing...I however thought I was in enemy territory but the Giants fans have long forgotten about the red shoed Rangers.  One fella passing by me said to his lady..."hey did you see that?  A Rangers fan.".

Oh. The. Humility.
